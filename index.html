WELCOME

Alameda Selfie League is a play at your own pace, on your own time pinball league with a finals tournament at the end of the season. The league is free to join and open to all skill levels. All you need are quarters and a device to take a selfie with your score!

The current season runs from May 1st to June 30th.
Finals will be held on July 1st at 3PM at Pacific Pinball Museum.

The next season runs from July 1st to August 31st.
Finals will be held on September 16th at 3PM at Pacific Pinball Museum.

How It Works

  1. Play from the list of pinball machines.
  2. Take a selfie with your score
  3. Submit your photo to Matchplay
  4. Get internationally ranked

Selfies

Score must be legible in photos for approval. Selfies must include yourself in the photo.

Game Submissions

Selfies must be submitted to Match Play within 24 hours of when the photo was taken.

Game Play

Extra balls are allowed. Death saves and bang backs are not.

Qualifying

You must submit scores on 4 machines to qualify.
Your top 6 scores from the eligible machines will determine seeding for the finals.
