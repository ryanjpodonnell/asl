WELCOME

Alameda Selfie League is a play at your own pace, on your own time pinball league with a finals tournament at the end of the season. The league is free to join and open to all skill levels. All you need are quarters and a device to take a selfie with your score!

The current season runs from May 1st to June 30th.
Finals will be held on July 1st at 3PM at Pacific Pinball Museum.
